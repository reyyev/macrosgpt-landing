Frequently Asked Questions

How does food logging work?

Just type what you ate in plain language (e.g. "chicken salad for lunch") or snap a photo of your plate. Mac, your AI coach, breaks down the macros instantly — no barcode scanning or database searching required.

How much does MacrosGPT cost?

MacrosGPT is $4.99/month with a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime through your Apple subscription settings. Basic features like workout tracking and grocery lists are available without a subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. Find MacrosGPT and tap Cancel. You can also use the link above under "Manage Your Subscription."

Is my data private?

Yes. Your nutrition and health data stays in your account and is never sold or shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does it sync with Apple Health?

Yes. MacrosGPT syncs with Apple Health to pull in workouts, steps, and activity data automatically — so you get both sides of the equation in one app.

What languages are supported?

MacrosGPT is available in 22 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, and more. Mac can understand food descriptions in your language.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. You can request one at reportaproblem.apple.com or contact us and we'll help guide you through the process.
