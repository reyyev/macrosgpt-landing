1. Introduction

MacrosGPT provides an AI-powered calorie and nutrition tracking application. This policy explains our data collection, use, storage, and protection practices.

By creating an account, you agree to data collection as described in this policy, including use for training and improving AI models.

This policy is designed to comply with App Store Requirements, CCPA, CPRA, GDPR, and global privacy standards.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email, authentication token, profile photo

Health & Fitness Data

  • Age, weight, height, activity level, goal weight
  • Nutrition logs, exercise logs, progress data

Camera & Photos

  • Food photos are stored temporarily and deleted after 24 hours

AI Chat Logs

  • Stored locally and deleted after 90 days
  • High-level insights may be cloud-stored

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type, usage events, crash logs, anonymous analytics

Sensitive Data

We do not collect biometric identifiers, government IDs, or financial information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • App functionality and personalization
  • AI improvement
  • Subscription management
  • Internal analytics
  • Customer support
  • Future marketing (with opt-out options)

4. AI Model Transparency

MacrosGPT is an independent product and is not affiliated with OpenAI or ChatGPT. AI assists with calorie estimation, food identification, insights, progress analysis, and coaching.

Data Access

The AI may access your age, weight, activity level, nutrition logs, exercise data, food photos, chat logs, and usage patterns to provide personalized coaching.

AI Training

By using MacrosGPT, you agree your data may be used to train AI models. Training data is anonymized, aggregated, and stripped of personal identifiers when possible.

Processing Locations

Data may be processed on cloud servers, Firebase infrastructure, or on-device.

Limitations

AI may misidentify foods, miscalculate calories, generate inaccurate advice, or hallucinate information. It does not provide medical advice.

Opt-Out

Opting out of AI data processing is not possible due to AI's core functionality role in the app.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Your data is never sold. Sharing occurs only with necessary service providers:

  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Authentication, database, and cloud functions
  • App Store Billing: Apple App Store and Google Play billing systems

No Google Analytics or Meta Analytics are used.

Legal disclosures may occur per law enforcement or court orders.

6. Data Storage & Retention

  • Cloud storage via Firebase: Retained until account deletion
  • Photos: Deleted after 24 hours
  • Chat logs: Deleted after 90 days
  • Account data: Deleted entirely upon account deletion, with backups removed within 30 days

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • HTTPS
  • Firebase security rules
  • Access logging
  • Role-based permissions

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8. International Data Transfers

Data is stored in the United States or European Union. EU transfers follow Standard Contractual Clauses and GDPR protections.

9. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR (EU)

If you are in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • File complaints with a supervisory authority

CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Opt-out of data sales (not applicable — we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

10. Children's Privacy

MacrosGPT is intended for ages 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover data from a child under 13, it will be deleted immediately.

11. Account Deletion

You may delete your account via the app or by emailing us. Upon deletion:

  • Cloud data is erased
  • Backups are removed within 30 days
  • Previously used AI training data is excluded prospectively
  • Photos and logs are auto-deleted per retention schedule

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be communicated via email or in-app notice.
