Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

MacrosGPT ("we", "us", or "our") operates the MacrosGPT mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Health & nutrition data: Food logs, macro targets, weight entries, and workout data you provide to the app.
  • Photos: Food photos you send are processed by AI to estimate nutrition. Photos are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide personalized nutrition coaching, track your progress, and improve the app. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase with encryption at rest and in transit. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud functions.
  • Google Gemini AI: Processes chat messages and food photos for nutrition estimation.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and billing.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@macrosgpt.app.

Children's Privacy

MacrosGPT is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
